# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is PRISM?

PRISM is a 10-phase methodology for building products from idea to launch.

## How long does it take?

Typically 8-16 weeks depending on project complexity.

## Can I use it for my project?

Yes! PRISM works for any type of project.

## Is it free?

Yes, PRISM is MIT licensed and completely free.

## How do I get started?

1. Read [GETTING_STARTED.md](../GETTING_STARTED.md)
2. Review [ETPD-SKILL.md](../ETPD-SKILL.md)
3. Prepare your project brief
4. Share with Claude AI
