## First Comment (Maker's Comment)

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm incredibly excited to unveil **PRISM**.

For the past few months, we’ve been building complex applications (like EduSync) entirely from scratch using autonomous AI agents. What we realized is that writing the code isn’t the hardest part anymore—it’s **structuring the architecture, planning the security, and knowing what to do next.** 

Without a roadmap, projects go completely off the rails or end in "tutorial hell."

**So we built a roadmap.** 

PRISM is a completely open-source methodology that breaks down development into **10 deterministic phases**:
1-2: Discovery & Design 
3-5: Architecture & Core Implementation
6: Security Hardening (Built-in OWASP compliance)
7-8: Infrastructure & Monitoring
9-10: Testing & Safe Launch

### 🤖 Why It Works With AI
We’ve formatted PRISM specifically so you can drop the `ETPD-SKILL.md` directly into Claude or ChatGPT along with your project brief, and the AI will autonomously guide you through every phase to completion.

### 🎁 What's Included?
Your free, MIT-licensed repository includes:
- The full 10-phase strategy
- Architecture and Security Checklists
- Project Brief Templates
- Case studies

I'd love to hear your thoughts, feedback, and anything you think is missing from the 10-phase flow! Let's build better products, faster. 🚀
