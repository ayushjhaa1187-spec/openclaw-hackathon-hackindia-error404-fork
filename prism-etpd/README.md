## What is PRISM?

PRISM is a **complete end-to-end product development methodology** that guides you from 
raw idea to production-ready launch in 10 structured phases.

✅ **Clear 10-Phase Roadmap** — Know exactly what to build and when  
✅ **Production-Grade Quality** — Security, testing, and monitoring from day 1  
✅ **Risk Mitigation** — Identify and address issues early  
✅ **Team Alignment** — Everyone knows the plan and their role  
✅ **Repeatable Process** — Use the same methodology for every project  
✅ **AI-Powered** — Works seamlessly with Claude AI for autonomous development  

## The 10 Phases

```text
Phase 1:  Discovery & Validation
Phase 2:  Product Design
Phase 3:  Architecture Design
Phase 4:  Project Setup
Phase 5:  Core Features
Phase 6:  Security & Quality
Phase 7:  Infrastructure & Deployment
Phase 8:  Monitoring & Operations
Phase 9:  Testing & QA
Phase 10: Go-Live & Launch
```
